When you import a key, you can choose to trust it based on the trust of other people in the web of trust. Keyserver: A keyserver is a server that stores GPG keys. When you import a key, you can choose to trust it based on the trust of the keyserver. Manually: You can also manually trust a GPG key by adding it to your keyring with the gpg –edit-key command.
